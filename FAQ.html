Frequently Asked Questions

BnR360 staffing services is a professional service that
helps businesses find, recruit, and hire qualified
employees on a temporary, temp-to-perm or permanent
basis.

BnR360 staffing services can help businesses save time
and money by taking care of the recruitment and hiring
process. We also provide access to a larger pool of
qualified candidates and provide flexibility to meet the
changing needs of the business.

The onboarding process with BnR360 staffing service typically begins with an initial consultation where the staffing agency will understand your business needs and the type of positions you need to fill. The agency will then present a pool of qualified candidates for you to review, interview, and select from. Once a candidate is selected, the agency will handle the hiring processes.

The cost of BnR360 staffing services vary depending on the type of staffing service you choose. There are four services to choose from. We have a fixed monthly subscription plan for each service. Contact us to know more about the Subscribtion plan.
